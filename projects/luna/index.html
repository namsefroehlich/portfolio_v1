About Luna

Led a three-member group project to build a Yelp-like platform for users to write, like, and comment on reviews of restaurants. Built with React and Redux, Django DRF, and PostgreSQL

React, Django, PostgreSQL, Docker

Challenge

Working in a Team, splitting the work in a time-efficient manner. Also, we had 1h a day with the TA's as a group coordinating this and our Gitlab-Branches was a challenge.

Solution

Having a Project Plan, and daily meetings helped us a lot for splitting the work and avoiding merge conflicts.
