About Auto-Graf

We developed a digital platform for Autograf & AutoSteiner Car Dealerships to interact with their customers with a Livechat experience which we built with Django-Channels and the Functionality for each salesman to have his individual page.

LIVE

React, Django, PostgreSQL, Docker

Challenge

Working with Django-Channels was the biggest challenge in this project. The TA's didn't know more than we did about Websockets, which made it significantly harder for us as a team.

Solution

Trial and Error is our keyword here. Everything related to Frontend and Backend was pretty straightforward as it was our third Project except Websockets (Django Channels), which took us weeks to learn and implement the LiveChat with its features. In the end, everything worked out but to finish the project we worked on it after the Bootcamp has ended.
